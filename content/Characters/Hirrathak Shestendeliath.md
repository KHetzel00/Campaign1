## Background

Being born golden the most was always expected of Hirrathak. His parents would beat him relentlessly in hopes of making him the perfect warrior, one fit to lead their clan. After years of abuse he ran away from home at the age of 12. 

Unsure of what to do with himself he turned to work as a guard, eventually being recruited by a Grandmaster Paladin of the Church of Bahamut. Ever since he joined he has never looked back on his old life, though his scars - both mental and physical - often bring themselves to bear.

## Background

Born to chromatic dragonborn parents Panjed has known discrimination his whole life. This culminated around the time he was 6 when a group of people in his hometown attacked his family in his home, he only managed to escape by crawling out through a broken window.

This escape left his body scarred and for days he wandered the wilderness before coming across a small Church of Bahamut. It was there he met Balasar, who took him in and trained him as an acolyte in the church.

Upon the discovery of the reemergence of the Cult of Tiamat Panjed grew inspired by a group of adventurers around his age to take up arms and defend that which was most important to him.

Since then he has been knighted as a paladin for the Church of Bahamut and frequently ventures out with his new friends and his master Hirrathak

## Relationships

Balasar Yarjerit: A close father figure to Panjed, he raised him and taught him the ways of the church.

Hirrathak Shestendeliath: Originally just a bully that would tease and harass Panjed for being chromatic, he has now become a mentor and somewhat close friend to Panjed, though he would never admit it

Akra Mystan: Panjeds Fiancé. Also a member of the Church.

Sorek Netty: Though Sorek is no longer with the party due to his 1 too many close calls with death, Panjed always held a respect for the Elf and enjoyed his company, though his did have to hold his nose sometimes.

Sir Jon: Panjed is perplexed by this paladin, nonetheless he considers him a valuable ally and friend

Sir Lan of Logil: Panjed finds Lan much more relatable than Jon, he is intrigued by his beliefs and sees him as one of the few voices of reason within the party

Zahir: Panjed still isn't sure what to think of this tabaxi, but as long as he's on his side he can't complain

Zellid: Panjed views Zellid with the upmost respect, he views him as the most responsible and frequently the most reasonable member of the party. Though they do not have a close personal relationship Panjed views Zellid as a close friend

Aquila: Originally put off by the necromancers magics, Panjed has come to respect Aquila as a powerful wizard and ally
